ENTRY REQUIREMENTS


UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS

Student Under National curriculam


SSC or Equivalent
  • Student must be from science background
  • Minimum GPA of 4.50 out of 5
  • Must have taken higher math

HSC or Equivalent
  • Student must be from science background
  • Minimum GPA of 4.50 out of 5
  • Regular student (no gap between SSC and HSC)
  • Must have A+ in following subjects
    1. MATH
    2. CHEMISTRY
    3. PHYSICS
  • Must have minimum A in following subjects
    1. English

O/A level or Equivalent


O level or Equivalent
  • Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and English with Minimum grade B

A level or Equivalent
  • Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry with Minimum grade A
  • Regular student (no gap between O and A level)

If the transcript provides both grade and marks, then the grades will be considered only for entry requirment. If the transcript shows only marks, only then marks will be converted into grades according to the following table

Technical Programs


Diploma in technical Education[DTE]

Duration: 1 year
  • The applicant must have at least Diploma in Engineering or equivalent with a total of at least 13 years of education

Bachelor of Science in Technical Education [BScTE]

Duration: 2 year
  • The applicant must have DTE.

Bachelor of Science in Technical Education [BScTE]

Duration: 1 year
  • The applicant must have Higher Diploma in Engineering or equivalent.


Postgraduate program



Master of Science in Engineering(M.Sc.Engg) / Master of Engineering(M.Engg)

  • BSc in engineering or equivalent in relevant field
  • Minimum CGPA of 2.5 (out of 4.0) in the BSc level


Postgraduate Diploma in Technical Education (PG DTE)

Duration: 1 year
  • The applicant must have B.Sc.TE or B.Sc.Engg. degree or equivalent in relevant field with a minimum CGPA of 2.5 (out of 4.0).


Master of Science in Technical Education (M.Sc. TE)

Duration: 2 year
  • The applicant must have B.Sc.TE or B.Sc.Engg. degree or equivalent in relevant field with a minimum CGPA of 2.5 (out of 4.0).


Master of Science in Technical Education (M.Sc. TE)

Duration: 1 year
  • The applicant must have PGDTE degree or equivalent in relevant field with a minimum CGPA of 2.5 (out of 4.0).


Doctor of Philosophy

  • The Master of Science in Technical Education MSc(TE) or Master of Education in TE
  • Must have minimum CGPA of 2.5 (out of 4.0) or 3.0 (out of 5.0) in both MSc and BSc levels
